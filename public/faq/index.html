What is a virtual try-on app?

A virtual try-on app lets shoppers upload a photo and preview how a garment may look on them before purchase. For Shopify fashion merchants, the real value is not novelty. It is reduced hesitation on the product page and a stronger sense of purchase confidence before checkout.

Does virtual try-on reduce returns?

It can. Virtual try-on is most useful when the shopper's uncertainty is about silhouette, styling, or how the garment may look on them. It should be positioned as one part of a broader returns-reduction stack, alongside strong product imagery, sizing guidance, and clear fit messaging.

How accurate is AI virtual try-on in 2026?

Accuracy is much better than the earlier generation of virtual fitting tools, but it still varies by garment, source image, and user photo quality. Merchants should evaluate the output on their own product catalogue instead of treating any provider claim as universal.

How much does a Shopify virtual try-on app cost?

That depends on pricing model and setup requirements. A lower sticker price can still be expensive if it produces weak outputs or demands a heavy operational workflow. For premium brands, total commercial fit matters more than the cheapest plan.

What is the difference between AR and AI virtual try-on?

AR tools tend to overlay products on a live camera feed. AI virtual try-on generates a new image based on the shopper and the garment. For full-garment fashion, AI is often the stronger route because it supports a more photorealistic merchandising experience.

Is virtual try-on safe with my photo?

Merchants should always check storage, retention, and processing policies. The right question is not whether a tool uses AI, but whether it explains the flow clearly and handles uploaded images responsibly.

Can I use virtual try-on on mobile?

Yes, and you should assume mobile matters. A large share of fashion traffic is mobile, so the upload flow and result page need to feel smooth on smaller screens if the feature is going to drive adoption.
